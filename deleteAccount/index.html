Delete Your Yogastraa Account

If you’d like to permanently delete your Yogastraa account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. In‑App Deletion

    • Open the Yogastraa app on your device.
    • Tap Settings from the Home screen.
    • Select Account.
    • Tap the Delete Profile button.
    • Confirm when prompted.

  2. What We Delete Immediately

    • Personal Profile Data: your name, email address, birth‑year, and gender.
    • User‑Generated Data: your bookmarks, playlists, session history, preferences, and Coach inputs.

  3. What We Keep (Anonymized Only)

    We retain only aggregate, anonymized analytics (via Firebase) for improving app performance and stability. This data cannot be traced back to you or your account.

  4. Need Help?

    If you experience any issues deleting your account, email us at contact@yogastraa.com with the subject line “Delete My Account” and we’ll assist you right away.
