High Blood Pressure: Calming Meals & What to Limit

By Team Yogastraa · 09/11/2025

Overview:
Dietary choices to support blood pressure alongside medical care.

Steps:
1) Emphasize: Potassium-rich plants (leafy greens, beans, bananas), whole grains, unsalted nuts.
2) Limit: Excess sodium, processed meats, heavy alcohol; favor home cooking.
3) Practice: Gentle sessions; long exhales; avoid strain and breath-holds.

Benefits:
• Supports cardiovascular health and recovery.

Precautions:
• Always follow prescribed medications; monitor BP.

Suggested Duration & Tips:
• Try spice/herb blends and citrus to cut salt; batch-cook beans and grains for fast meals.
