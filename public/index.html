What is Yogastraa?

Yogastraa is the world’s first AI-powered yoga learning platform that combines traditional yoga science with intelligent coaching. Designed for beginners and advanced practitioners alike, Yogastraa delivers step-by-step asanas, pranayama, mudras and guided meditations—now enhanced by AI personalization and in-app chat guidance.

With an easy-to-use interface and a comprehensive multimedia library (photos, videos, and tutorials), Yogastraa helps you improve flexibility, reduce stress, gain strength, and build a consistent practice. Our intelligent features tailor sessions to your age, goals, time availability and fitness level for truly personalized progress.

Key features include: AI Acharya chat mentor, AI-generated coach sessions, 400+ asana demos, pranayama & mudra guides, therapeutic treatment programs, playlists & bookmarks, and an analytics dashboard to track progress and recommendations.

Asanas – The Postures of Perfection

Yoga asanas, or postures, are at the heart of Yogastraa. Our app provides a wealth of information about each asana, including its origin, meaning, and pronunciation. You can explore the etymology and alternate names of each posture, along with its benefits, contraindications, and common mistakes to avoid.

For every asana, Yogastraa offers step-by-step instructions, alignment tips, variations, and modifications. High-quality images and video tutorials make it easy to understand and follow the practices. Whether you’re working on improving flexibility, strength, or balance, Yogastraa’s asana section ensures you’re guided every step of the way.

Pranayama – The Breath of Life

Pranayama, the yogic art of breath control, is a cornerstone of a balanced and peaceful mind. Yogastraa delves deep into this practice by offering detailed information about various pranayamas, including their meaning, benefits, and proper techniques.

With comprehensive tutorials, Yogastraa teaches you how to harness the power of your breath to achieve better physical and mental health. Each pranayama is accompanied by images and video guides, ensuring you perform these practices safely and effectively. Whether your goal is to enhance lung capacity, reduce stress, or find inner calm, Yogastraa’s pranayama section is your ultimate guide.

Mudras – Unlocking Energy Channels

Mudras, or hand gestures, play a vital role in channeling energy and enhancing the effectiveness of yoga practices. Yogastraa’s mudra section is a treasure trove of knowledge, providing detailed descriptions of each mudra, their origins, and their significance in yoga and meditation.

Our app offers clear instructions, benefits, contraindications, and variations for every mudra. Accompanied by high-quality images and video tutorials, the mudra section empowers you to incorporate these subtle yet powerful practices into your daily routine. Unlock your body’s hidden potential with Yogastraa’s guidance.

Meditation – The Path to Inner Peace

Meditation is a journey inward, and Yogastraa serves as your perfect companion on this path. The app offers extensive resources on various meditation techniques, including their objectives, mental aptitude requirements, and step-by-step processes.

Through clear explanations, images, and video tutorials, Yogastraa ensures you achieve mindfulness and inner tranquility. Whether you seek to enhance focus, relieve stress, or achieve spiritual growth, our meditation section provides everything you need to get started and sustain your practice.

Coach — AI-Guided Yoga, 24/7

Imagine a personal yoga teacher available 24/7. With AI Acharya, Yogastraa delivers intelligent coaching through an interactive chat: ask questions, get posture cues, request breathwork guidance, or generate a full session instantly.

Our AI Coach builds personalized routines (warm-up ➜ asanas ➜ pranayama ➜ mudra ➜ meditation) based on your profile and goals. Free guided sessions are available to all users; Premium unlocks advanced, AI-curated treatment programs, deeper analytics, and full session exports for repeat practice.

Whether you want stress relief, flexibility, or therapeutic support, Yogastraa’s AI-driven coach adapts as you improve—making yoga learning smarter, safer, and more consistent.
